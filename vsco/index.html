Being a multi-disciplined designer, I love collaborating with design and dev teams, nurturing creative ideas within the design process, and working iteratively to promote quality outcomes based on learning.

Design direction

Over the course of my career (and ever since I discovered the internet), the form and function of digital experiences has been a key focus of mine. Much like the evolving digital landscape, my design career, processes, and influences, have also evolved.

My understanding of design principles, UX methodologies, and technology, have given me a foundation to adapt and respond to the changing landscape. These hands-on skills have also enabled me to define new processes and standards within the Australian digital design industry that focus on workflow, quality design, and user experience – from flash micro sites in 2005, to responsive web design, multi-platform product design, and new and emerging technologies.

I have also had the chance to lead design teams within start-up studios, large agencies and in-house software companies. Along with servicing clients, brands and products, a large part of my role is dedicated to cultural change and leadership. As a design leader, my motivations are to encourage, support and mentor designers around me, while also giving direction on process and cultural improvements to build trust and promote design thinking within companies.
