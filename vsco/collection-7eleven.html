7-Eleven

Product design, concept & direction

Brief

Multi-platform TV Guide

Design a TV guide across multiple platforms to integrate with existing home entertainment systems so that subscribers can browse, watch and record their shows on-the-go, connect to social media channels, and access additional content from their phone or tablet.

Role

Design Director

I was responsible for leading a multi-disciplined design team through various activities from concept design, user experience, and execution, while also driving the product vision forward alongside the product owner and key Foxtel stakeholders.

Process

Concept, Sprint, Delivery

The project was setup as a nine week Agile and Scrum delivery whereby design production ran in parallel with development. Pre-planning, architecture and concept design were undertaken prior to this which gave the team an opportunity to research, explore and test ideas.

iOS 7 iPhone, iPad & Android 4.4+

Since launching, The Foxtel TV Guide for iPhone, iPad and Android has generated over 1.2 million individual downloads, whilst averaging a 4 star rating across both app stores.
