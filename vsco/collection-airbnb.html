Airbnb

Design for trust and global communities

Trust

An emotional bond

Trust is one of the hardest things to manufactor. It's an emotional pact between two people, and in this case, total strangers travelling around the world to stay in one anothers home. Trust is also hard to get back when broken.

Perception

Transparency

In the abscense of knowledge, good faith is often misplaced with scepticism. Designing for perception is more about being as transparent as possible so that hosts and guests can come to an approriate conclusion about one another.

Identity

Trust at scale

Identification is a pillar within any community. Its used as a foundational tool and safety net in the event of something going wrong. Identity at airbnb has decrease more incidences than any other product.
