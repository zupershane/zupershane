Woolworths retail

Product design, concept & direction

Brief

Shopping from your pocket

Extend the current functionality of the Woolworths iPhone app to create a seamless shopping experience both online and in-store. Integrated shopping lists, intuitive product finder, trolley, checkout, and even options to collect in-store.

Role

Design Director

While working closely with the client on the vision for the new product direction, I also led a small design and user experience team from initial concept design and prototyping, through to development, QA and launch over a period of 12 months.

Process

UX & integration

The Woolworths program of work had been running for 2 years prior to my involvement. The extended functionality required a complete redesign of the existing product to create a cohesive user experience while integrating with complex backend systems.

User centred design

Honest fact: Although the design process from concept to execution was very user centric, the final app that launched to market was met with a very negative response amongst it's 7 million users. Ongoing efforts are being made to align the product with the expectations of it's users, but poor business decisions and product direction still persist.
